Professional Summary

Full Professor of Public Economics at the Department of Statistics and Quantitative Methods, University of Milano-Bicocca. My research focuses on health economics, statistical evaluation of healthcare systems, policy evaluation, and the application of modern econometric and machine learning methods to administrative data.
